\section{Roles and responsibilities}
\label{sec:b}

Three roles share a single response.

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=3.4em,itemsep=3pt]
\item[\texttt{Lead}] directs the response and decides escalation
\item[\texttt{Assist}] executes the immediate safe-state action
\item[\texttt{Reserve}] documents the event and prepares the report
\end{itemize}
